Daniel Miller sells Williamsburg multifamily building for $3,100,000

Mendel Gold acquired a 5-unit walk-up building located at 154 N 9th St in Williamsburg, Brooklyn for $3,100,000 ($827/ft & $620,000/unit). The building size is 3,750 square feet. The building is located on N 9th St just off the corner of Bedford Ave.

No mortgage was obtained to finance the transaction. The deal went into contract on 1/28/2020, closed on 8/25/2020, and was recorded on 9/11/2020.
